# Market Analysis: Dutch Wtp + EU Pension AI Opportunities

> Added: 2026-03-05 | Source: Jonathan (founder research) | Status: Under evaluation

## The Disruption Window

Two structural forces converging:
1. **Dutch Wtp**: €1,500B in collective pension pots → personal pots. ~9.5M workers transitioned Jan 2026, €900B more by 2027-2028.
2. **EU SIU reforms** (Dec 2025): 19 pieces of legislation amended. PEPP revision, auto-enrolment, pension dashboards, cross-border portability.

## Opportunities Ranked

### Opportunity 1: Wtp Transition Tooling for Employers & Advisors (9/10)
- **Problem**: ~50,000 pension schemes must transfer. Capacity shortages at DNB, administrators, actuaries, advisors.
- **Customer**: SMB employers, small pension advisors — fragmented, urgent deadlines, underserved.
- **What AI does**: Transition plan generation, employee communication automation, works council Q&A, compliance monitoring.
- **Revenue**: €200-€1,000/mo per employer. One-time €2K-€10K for larger firms.
- **Deadline pressure**: Oct 2026 (insurers), Jan 2028 (funds).
- **Why us**: Nobody building lightweight AI tooling for SMB segment. Enterprise tools (Keylane, Maatwerk) don't serve the long tail.
- **Edge**: Jonathan is Dutch, understands ZZP landscape, building AI-first for small businesses.

### Opportunity 2: Personal Pension Dashboard / Advisory (7.5/10)
- **Problem**: Workers now own investment risk. Never had to think about pension as investment.
- **What AI does**: Aggregate Pillar 1+2+3, scenario modeling, life-event triggers, cross-border scenarios.
- **Caveat**: AFM restrictions on financial advice are tight. Must stay in "information/education" or get licensed.
- **Competition**: MijnPensioenoverzicht.nl exists but basic.

### Opportunity 3: Cross-Border Pension & PEPP Infrastructure (7/10, longer runway)
- **Problem**: Pensions not portable across EU. Only 2 PEPP providers exist.
- **What AI does**: Cross-border optimizer, PEPP comparison engine, employer PEPP integration.
- **Caveat**: Too early — PEPP adoption low, tax harmonization incomplete. Position now, monetize later.

### Opportunity 4: Pension Fund Operations AI (8/10 B2B)
- **Problem**: Pension funds spending massively on Wtp transition. Legacy admin systems groaning.
- **What AI does**: Invaren calculation verification, participant comms at scale, regulatory reporting, dispute resolution.
- **Caveat**: Enterprise buyers, long procurement cycles. Advisor channel more accessible.

## Strategic Positioning

- Build for Dutch Wtp first (immediate revenue, deadline pressure)
- Generalize for EU market as SIU reforms roll out
- Netherlands = guinea pig. Whatever works here becomes template for 27 member states.
- EU households save 15% (vs 3% US) but in low-yield deposits. Multi-trillion-euro shift over next decade.
